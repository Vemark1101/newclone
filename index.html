Event Overview ★

SMCC-CCIS Week 2025 is an annual campus event that highlights student projects, industry talks, and hands-on workshops about software development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and more.

Join us for a week of learning, networking, and fun as we celebrate the power of technology in education & innovation.
